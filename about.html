About LHMA

At Little Hands Montessori Academy, we are dedicated to nurturing young minds and fostering a lifelong love of learning. With two vibrant locations—one nestled in the bustling city of Calgary and the other in the charming town of Okotoks, Alberta—we bring the renowned Montessori method to families in our community.

Our Montessori Approach

Our guiding philosophy is grounded in the time-tested Montessori approach, which empowers children to become confident, self-motivated learners. In our carefully prepared environments, children are encouraged to explore, question, and discover at their own pace, cultivating a deep sense of independence and a thirst for knowledge.

A Unique Learning Experience

At both our Calgary and Okotoks locations, you'll find inviting, child-centric spaces designed to inspire curiosity and creativity. Our dedicated Montessori-trained educators serve as compassionate guides, supporting each child's individual growth and development. With mixed-age classrooms, we foster a sense of community and mentorship among our students, helping them build strong relationships and develop essential life skills.

Exploring Nature and Beyond

We believe in the importance of connecting children with the world around them. Both our locations feature outdoor spaces where children can engage with nature, fostering an appreciation for the environment and a sense of wonder. We also offer a well-rounded curriculum that includes the arts, practical life activities, language, math, and cultural studies.

Parent/Guardian Partnership

At Little Hands Montessori Academy, we view parents as essential partners in their child's educational journey. We encourage open communication, regular parent-teacher conferences, and opportunities for involvement in our vibrant school community.

We Use Hi Mama!

The HiMama app bridges communication between educators and family members. With HiMama, families receive real-time updates about their child, including:

  • Photos & Videos
  • Daily Reports & Activity Schedules
  • Developmental Observations & Assessments
  • Family Messaging & Newsletters
  • ..and much more!

Safety and Excellence

Rest assured that your child's safety and well-being are our top priorities. Our facilities are maintained to the highest standards, and we adhere to all licensing and accreditation requirements.

Join Our Montessori Family

We invite you to explore our Calgary and Okotoks locations and discover the difference a Montessori education can make in your child's life. At Little Hands Montessori Academy, we're not just a daycare; we're a community dedicated to nurturing the potential within every child.

Contact us today to schedule a visit and learn more about how we can support your child's growth and development on their unique educational journey.
